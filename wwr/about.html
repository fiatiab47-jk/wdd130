White Water Rafting Company

As a water rafting company, our mission is to bring adventure to life through thrilling and safe river rafting experiences for families, friends, students and explorers of all skill levels. We organize trips, provide the watercraft, life jackets and everything you need to feel safe embarking on this trip. We intend to provide a trilling, safe, and a memorable for all our guest, regardless of age or skill level. Our expect guides and state-of-the-art equipment ensures you can focus on enjoying the breathtaking scenery and exhilarating rapids, while we take care of the rest.

History

With over a decade of experience navigating the mighty rivers of the Volta Region of Ghana, our team at White Water Rafting Company has established itself as a leader in providing unforgettable river rafting adventures. From our humble beginnings in 2002 we have been passionate about sharing the thrill of rafting with enthusiasts and newcomers alike, while prioritizing safety, sustainability, and exceptional customer service.
